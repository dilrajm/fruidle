How to Play

Use the fruit keyboard to guess fruit sequence in 5 tries! Guesses must be 3, 5 or 7 fruits long depending on the level. The colour of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the fruit sequence.

Examples

The pear is in the sequence and in the right spot.

The melon is in the sequence but in the wrong spot.

The kiwifruit is not in the sequence in any spot.
